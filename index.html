Who We Are

We are a group of developers with passion to develop within the best code.

A small group of technicians with passion for IT started to work in 2019 with Python, making them a workgroup of developers able to learn and, able to improve their technique, even on some cases able to process request from different enviorments and customers.

What, How, When, Where and With what

First step is the definition of what's is required, Which technical aspects are needed to take into consideration, When needs to be delivered? What time line do we have?, Where needs to be stored? IDE? Cloud?, Which language is the best for this kind of request

Design

Once do we know exactly what is expected from our side, team starts to design and to set in place a coordination to be able to deliver the product.

Build

Once all the mechanism of coordination are in place, all the team start to works together on the request. Doing checkpoints with application owner

Launch

Before target date, team will provide the needed package and will be present during the testing
